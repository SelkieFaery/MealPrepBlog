Welcome to my meal prep blog!
I have only been meal prepping for lunches for a few months, but I have learned a lot along the way. I have spent a lot of time researching new meal ideas online, some great, some not so great. But one thing I have noticed about all of the meal prep blogs is they all follow one format, "Here is a meal I like, this is why I like it, this is how I make it, recipe at the bottom". This blog is not meant to share my recipes, but rather reviewing recipes I have tried.I am going to blog all of my experiences making different lunch prep and other recipes I have tried, including the good, the bad, and the ugly. Instead of including details on how to make something with the recipe at the bottom, I am going to review the recipes I make, including what I liked, what I didn't like, what I did wrong, what I did right, and any ideas I can think of to make it better.
